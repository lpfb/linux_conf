>Compression syntax:
- tar -czvf <compressed file anem>.tar.gz <directory/file name>

>>>Ex: Compressing an entire directory (microblaze_test.sdk):
    * tar -czvf sdk_files.tar.gz microblaze_test.sdk

> Decompression syntax:
    * tar -xzvf <tar ball name>.tar.gz

>>>Ex: Decompressing a file/directory:
    * tar -xzvf <tar ball name>.tar.gz
